Kid vs. Conan: Miraculous Airwalking

When an obnoxious old man issues Kid a challenge to steal his blue jewel, Kid comes up with a scheme that will make it look like he is walking on air. With the help of his Butler and Chopper #7, Kid is able to sneak through more than 20 helicopters without any of them seeing the wires attached to his back. However Kid has another challenge in the crowd that makes things difficult. None other than Detective Conan is on the scene. Conan recognizes kid impersonating the old man and waits in his escape car. When Kid tries to escape he unknowingly takes Conan with him. When Conan reveals himself, he has his tranq gun pointed right at kid. Luckily Kid has another escape planned, a trick he only uses when his fuel line gets shot by the tranq gun.
